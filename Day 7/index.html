Featured Berger Breeds

Berger Blanc Suisse

The Berger Blanc Suisse is known for its intelligence and loyalty, making it an excellent hunting companion.

Berger Picard

The Berger Picard is a sturdy and agile breed, ideal for hunting in various terrains.

Berger des Pyrénées

Also known as the Pyrenean Shepherd, this breed comes in two varieties: rough-faced and smooth-faced. They are agile, energetic herding dogs with a strong work ethic.

Berger de Beauce

Also known as the Beauceron, this breed is large, powerful, and intelligent. They are versatile working dogs often used for herding, guarding, and various dog sports.

Berger de Brie

Also known as the Briard, this breed is characterized by its long, flowing coat and distinctive beard. Briards are intelligent, loyal, and protective, making them excellent herding and guard dogs.

Berger de l'Atlas

Also known as the Atlas Shepherd or Moroccan Sheepdog, this breed originates from the Atlas Mountains of Morocco.
